<h3>Overview</h3>

I developed this website to organize, manage and modify recipes. I found many online recipes frustrating due to intrusive ads, lengthy introductions and inconsistent formatting. This project centralizes recipes in a single, structured location. It also supports recipe adjustments using AI to meet specific requirements, such as scaling quantities or altering ingredients. When an adjustment is made, a new variation of that recipe is created. So it is easy to keep track and compare different versions of the same recipe.
