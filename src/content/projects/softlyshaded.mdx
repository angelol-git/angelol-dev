<h3 class="text-sm text-gray-400 uppercase tracking-widest text-muted-foreground">
  Overview
</h3>

Worked with Softly Shaded to consolidate their lash and brow services, pricing, and FAQ into a website.

Softly Shaded's clients primarily found them through Instagram and Facebook, but had a problem of scattered information across posts and stories. The website serves as a professional landing point that streamlines the booking process by presenting all essential business information in one accessible location.

<h3 class="text-sm text-gray-400 uppercase tracking-widest text-muted-foreground">
  Notable Tech Used
</h3>

<h4>Astro</h4>

- A static site generator was used for high performance and simplicity
- Repetitive designs in pricing tables and FAQs made it ideal for component based templating
- Astro Content Collections organized services and FAQs in structured markdown files, no CMS needed for content that changes infrequently
- More complex technologies like Next or React was unnecessary due to limited interactivity
